Pre calculations and component selection

First we need to calculate momentum and power before selecting motors. We assume 50% losses in the motors (because of poor design). Helpful link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=idVcItHfGS4&t=3s (youtube video on how to run stepper motor) https://www.instructables.com/Complete-Motor-Guide-for-Robotics/ Stepper motors need to be powerfull, servo motors are better.

Hardware required

  • Arduino Uno
  • 2 big stepper motors MST001A-S8
  • medium stepper motor SM42HT46-1684B
  • 1 small stepper motor SY35ST26-0248A
  • Motor controller TB660
  • Motor controller Velleman VMA409
  • Motor controller TB6612
  • Power suply
  • Bread board
  • hook-up wires
